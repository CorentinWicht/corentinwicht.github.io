Corentin Wicht, MsC.

corentin.wicht@unifr.ch
I am a cognitive neuroscientist investigating the neural correlates of expectations effects. Using dedicated designs, I am able to distinguish between pure pharmacological effects of a substance separately from the mere effects of expectations. In parallel, I am developping MATLAB- and R-based automated data preprocessing, analysis and visualization tools.
